Xingbo Yuan is an Associate Professor at the Institute of Particle Physics, Central China Normal University. His research area is **particle physics theory**.

He received his Ph.D. from **Central China Normal University** in 2014, after completing his bachelor's degree at **Zhengzhou University** in 2008.

Before joining Central China Normal University in 2018, he worked as a postdoctoral researcher at **Yonsei University** and the **National Center for Theoretical Sciences (Taiwan)**, and also served as a visiting scholar at the **Korea Institute for Advanced Study**.
